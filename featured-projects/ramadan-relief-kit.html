Ramadan Relief Kit Distribution

Ramadan Initiative

A humanitarian initiative organized to support underprivileged families during the holy month of Ramadan by distributing essential grocery kits. This effort ensures that families can observe Ramadan with dignity and without financial stress.

Beneficiaries: 16 families
Total Support Value: ₹25,000 worth of essential grocery items

Organized by: Jasoor Welfare Foundation (JWF), Melathaniyam
Supported by: Association of Muslim Professionals (AMP)

Volunteers personally delivered the kits to families in need, spreading compassion, unity, and the true spirit of giving during Ramadan.
