Ramadan Special Lecture

Ramadan Event

Program Title:Ramadan Special Lecture

Topic: Building Future Leaders Through Education, Skills, and Community

Date: Saturday, February 15, 2026

Time: 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Venue: SAR Hall, Melathaaniyam

Speakers: Imam R. Hussain Baji (SDPI), Youth Mentors, Community Leaders

Organizer: Jasoor Welfare Foundation (JWF)

The Ramadan Special Lecture is crafted from the event posters to deliver a transformative day of insight, training, and connection for young members of our community. It focuses on practical leadership development, goal-setting, and faith-driven community service.

Interactive sessions include mentorship panels, group workshops, and peer networking to ensure all attendees leave with actionable plans and renewed purpose. The content is anchored in local values and global best practices.

Jasoor Welfare Foundation is committed to fostering resilient, confident youth. We create structured follow-up pathways so participants can continue personal growth beyond the summit through ongoing programs and community support.
