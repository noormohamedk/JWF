Who We Are

A Foundation Built on
Service & Compassion

Jasoor Welfare Foundation is a non-governmental organization dedicated to fostering peace, protecting human rights, and driving sustainable development. Since our founding, we have worked hand-in-hand with communities to create lasting change from the ground up.

Our programs span education, women empowerment, youth development, and humanitarian aid — all grounded in the belief that every individual deserves dignity and opportunity.

Our Mission

To empower marginalized communities through inclusive programs that restore dignity and create sustainable livelihoods.

Our Vision

A just and peaceful society where every individual has access to rights, education, and opportunity.

What We Stand For

Our Core Focus Areas

Everything we do is guided by these foundational pillars that shape our programs, partnerships, and advocacy efforts.

Peace & Reconciliation

Promoting peaceful coexistence, conflict resolution, and community dialogue to build harmonious societies.

Human Rights

Advocating for the fundamental rights and dignity of all people, regardless of gender, ethnicity, or background.

Sustainable Development

Implementing long-term programs that address root causes of poverty through economic empowerment and capacity building.

Education Access

Ensuring quality education reaches the most vulnerable children and youth, regardless of circumstance.

Youth Leadership

Cultivating the next generation of leaders through mentorship, skills training, and civic engagement.

Women's Empowerment

Advancing gender equality and women's economic, social, and political participation at all levels.

Our Programs

What We Do on the Ground

Our grassroots programs are designed to address the most pressing needs of communities while building resilient systems for lasting impact.

01

Education & Scholarship Support

Scholarships, school infrastructure support, teacher training, and digital literacy programs for underprivileged students.

02

Youth Empowerment Initiatives

Skills training, entrepreneurship bootcamps, mentorship programs, and civic engagement for young adults aged 16–30.

03

Community Health Programs

Health awareness camps, mental health support, maternal care, and clean water access programs in rural areas.

04

Social Impact Research

Publishing evidence-based research and policy briefs to inform government and NGO strategies for greater community impact.

05

Humanitarian Relief

Emergency food, medical aid, shelter, and rehabilitation support for disaster-affected and conflict-displaced communities.

06

Women Empowerment Programs

Vocational training, microfinancing, legal aid, and leadership development to uplift women in underserved communities.
